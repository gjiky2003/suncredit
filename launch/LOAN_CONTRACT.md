## 6. DEFAULT AND LATE FEES

### 6.1 Late Fee
If you don't pay within 10 days of the due date, you will be charged a $25 late fee (the lesser of $25 or 5% of the payment per TFC §342.255).

### 6.2 What Counts as Default
- Missing a payment by more than 30 days
- Bankruptcy or insolvency
- Providing false information on your application
- Death (legal estate handles)

### 6.3 What Happens If You Default
- We may demand the entire balance immediately (acceleration)
- We will report to credit bureaus
- We may send the account to collections (using lawful methods only)
- We may file suit in [COUNTY] County, Texas, for the unpaid amount + reasonable attorney fees + court costs

### 6.4 Cure Right
If you cure (pay all overdue amounts + late fees) before charge-off, the loan resumes normal status.

You may dispute any charge or billing error by writing to us within 60 days. We will investigate per TILA Section 161.
