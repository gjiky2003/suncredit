# SunCredit — Surety Bond Procurement Guide

## What Is a Surety Bond?
A surety bond is a financial guarantee from a third-party surety company that SunCredit will comply with state lending laws. If we breach, the bond compensates harmed consumers.

## Required Amount
- **Texas OCCC:** $25,000 minimum for Regulated Lenders
- Some other states require more (Florida $25k, California $25k+, NY varies)

## Pricing
For most applicants with reasonable credit:
- **Annual premium:** 1-3% of bond amount
- **$25k bond:** ~$250-$750/year
- **$100k bond:** ~$1,000-$3,000/year

## Top Providers
1. **Surety Bonds Direct** (suretybonds.com)
2. **JW Surety Bonds** (jwsuretybonds.com)
3. **NFP Surety** (nfp.com)
4. **Lance Surety Bonds** (lancesuretybonds.com)

## How to Apply
1. Get quote (15 min online)
2. Submit application: business info + principal credit report
3. Bond issued same-day or next-day if approved
4. File bond with Texas OCCC via ALECS portal

## Common Issues
- Poor personal credit → higher premium (5-10% instead of 1-3%)
- New business → some carriers require 1-year-old company
- High-risk industry rating → still get bond, just costlier

## Action
Get 3 quotes and pick the best. Budget $750/year for first year.
