# SunCredit Lending LLC — LLC Operating Agreement

**This Operating Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] by and among the undersigned member(s) of **SunCredit Lending LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company (the "Company").

## ARTICLE 1 — FORMATION
1.1 **Name.** The Company is "SunCredit Lending LLC".
1.2 **Jurisdiction.** Formed under the Delaware Limited Liability Company Act.
1.3 **Principal Office.** [STREET ADDRESS, CITY, STATE, ZIP]

1.5 **Purpose.** Consumer lending, financial technology, AI-powered underwriting, and any lawful business activity.
1.6 **Term.** Perpetual unless dissolved per Article 8.

## ARTICLE 2 — MEMBERS
2.1 **Initial Member.** [YOUR NAME], holding 100% membership interest.
2.2 **Capital Contribution.** Initial contribution of $[AMOUNT] in cash.
2.3 **Additional Contributions.** May be made by unanimous written consent.
2.4 **No Right to Withdraw Capital.** No member may withdraw capital without unanimous member consent.

## ARTICLE 3 — MANAGEMENT
3.1 **Manager-Managed.** The Company is manager-managed.
3.2 **Initial Manager.** [YOUR NAME] is appointed as the initial Manager.
3.3 **Powers.** Manager has full authority to:
- (a) Bind the Company in contracts
- (b) Open/manage bank accounts and merchant processor accounts (Stripe, Plaid)
- (c) Hire and fire employees and contractors
- (d) Originate, service, and collect loans
- (e) Set lending criteria and risk policies (subject to applicable law)
- (f) Engage legal counsel, accountants, auditors

3.4 **Limitations.** Manager may not without written member consent:
- (a) Sell substantially all assets
- (b) Merge or dissolve the Company
- (c) Incur debt exceeding $[LIMIT]
- (d) Distribute assets in liquidation

## ARTICLE 4 — ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS
4.1 **Profits and Losses.** Allocated proportionally to membership interests.
4.2 **Distributions.** Made at Manager's discretion subject to:
- Reserves for loan loss provisions (CECL)
- Operating capital requirements
- Regulatory capital requirements
- Outstanding obligations
4.3 **Tax Treatment.** Single-member LLC taxed as disregarded entity (or check box for S-Corp election).

## ARTICLE 5 — BOOKS AND RECORDS
5.1 The Company shall maintain:
- Complete financial books per GAAP
- Loan portfolio performance data (CECL-compliant)
- Regulatory filings and correspondence
- Audit trail for all material decisions
- Borrower records (5+ year retention per regulatory requirements)
5.2 Members entitled to inspect books upon reasonable notice.

## ARTICLE 6 — INDEMNIFICATION
6.1 The Company shall indemnify the Manager and members for acts taken in good faith and in the best interests of the Company.
6.2 No indemnification for fraud, willful misconduct, or knowing violation of law.

## ARTICLE 7 — TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTERESTS
7.1 No member may transfer membership interest without written consent of all members.
7.2 Right of first refusal for remaining members on any proposed transfer.
7.3 Death/incapacity: interest passes to estate; estate may sell to remaining members at fair value.

## ARTICLE 8 — DISSOLUTION
8.1 Dissolution events:
- Unanimous consent of members
- Judicial dissolution
- Event causing dissolution under Delaware law
8.2 Upon dissolution, assets distributed:
- (a) Pay creditors (including loan obligations to investors)
- (b) Establish reserve for pending claims
- (c) Distribute remainder to members per their interests

## ARTICLE 9 — MISCELLANEOUS
9.1 **Governing Law.** Delaware
9.2 **Amendments.** Require written consent of all members
9.3 **Severability.** Invalid provisions severed; remainder enforceable
9.4 **Entire Agreement.** This document supersedes all prior agreements
