## Critical Negotiation Points

1. **Loan ownership** — does the bank hold or sell to you immediately? (Affects capital needs)
2. **Underwriting authority** — must their compliance officer approve every model change?
3. **State coverage** — confirm all 50 states or specific list
4. **Volume minimums** — most have monthly minimums after ramp (push for low/no minimum first 6 months)
5. **Termination terms** — 90-day vs 180-day notice; loan portfolio handling on exit
6. **Exclusivity** — avoid signing exclusive (gives you no leverage)
