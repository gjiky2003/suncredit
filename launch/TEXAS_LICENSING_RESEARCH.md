# SunCredit — Texas OCCC Regulated Lender License

## Overview
Texas requires a Regulated Lender License under Chapter 342, Texas Finance Code, for non-depository personal lenders charging APRs above 10%.

## Licensing Authority
- **Office of Consumer Credit Commissioner (OCCC)**
- 2601 N. Lamar Blvd, Austin, TX 78705
- 512-936-7600 · occc.texas.gov

## Application Steps
1. **Create ALECS account** — occc.texas.gov/alecs (15 min)
2. **Pay application fee** — $1,000 non-refundable
3. **Surety bond** — $25,000 minimum (~$250-500/year premium)
4. **Background check + credit report** — for each principal
5. **Submit business plan** — 11-section compliance plan (we have this)
6. **Submit proposed loan contract** — plain-language per TAC Ch 90 (we have this)
7. **Wait for review** — typically 4-6 weeks

## Timeline
- Apply: Day 1
- Background/credit pull: Day 2-7
- OCCC review: Day 14-45
- License issued: Day 28-60

## NMLS Note
Some Texas licensing is transitioning to NMLS (Nationwide Multistate Licensing System). Check current process at occc.texas.gov before filing.
