## COMPANY OVERVIEW

SunCredit originates personal loans of $500-$50,000 to U.S. consumers using AI-native underwriting. The platform combines:
- Machine learning ensemble (AUC 0.71) on traditional credit features
- Cash flow analysis (90-day bank transactions) for thin-file borrowers
- Sub-second decisioning with explainable risk reasons
- Fully-automated origination, servicing, and collections

**Target borrower:** FICO 580-759, $25k-$150k income, looking for $1k-$15k loans for debt consolidation, home improvement, medical, etc.
