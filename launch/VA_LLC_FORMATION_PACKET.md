## Step 2: File Articles of Organization (Form LLC-1011)

**Where:** Virginia State Corporation Commission — https://cis.scc.virginia.gov
**Cost:** $100 (one-time)
**Processing:** ~1 business day online

### Required fields (fill these in)

| Field | Your value |
|---|---|
| Entity name | SunCredit Lending, LLC |
| Principal office address | _<your VA home or business address>_ |
| Registered agent | _<you, or a service like Northwest Registered Agent ~$125/yr>_ |
| Registered agent address | _<must be a VA street address — not PO box>_ |
| Organizer name | _<your full legal name>_ |
| Organizer signature | _<e-signature on file portal>_ |

**Do this yourself online — no lawyer needed for this step.**

## Step 3: Get EIN from IRS

**Where:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
**Cost:** Free
**Time:** 10 minutes online

You'll need this for: business bank account, Stripe, payroll, taxes, lender partnerships.

---

Recommended (fintech-friendly, fast approval, online-only):

| Bank | Approval time | Best for |

You'll need: EIN, Articles of Organization PDF, your driver's license, proof of address.

---

## Step 5: File BOI Report (Beneficial Ownership)

**Required by federal law (FinCEN) since 2024.**
**Where:** https://boiefiling.fincen.gov
**Cost:** Free
**Deadline:** Within 90 days of LLC formation

Report yourself (and any other ≥25% owner) as beneficial owner. Skipping this = $500/day penalty.

## What this does NOT give you

❌ The right to lend money to strangers — that requires either (a) state lending licenses or (b) a bank partnership. See `LENDER_PARTNERSHIP_OUTREACH.md`.

✅ The right to take "loans" from friends/family as private agreements — fine for the pilot phase.
