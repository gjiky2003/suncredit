Privacy Policy

GLBA-compliant · Last updated January 1, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental.

We follow the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA) and provide this notice describing how we collect, share, and protect your nonpublic personal information.

Information we collect

  • Identity: name, date of birth, SSN (last 4), address, phone, email
  • Financial: income, employment, debts, credit reports, bank account details
  • Transactional: payments, account history, cash-flow data (with your consent)
  • Technical: device, IP address, browser, cookies

How we use it

To underwrite, originate, and service your loan; to verify identity and prevent fraud; to comply with law (BSA, OFAC, FCRA); and to improve our products.

Sharing

We share information only with: (a) consumer-reporting agencies; (b) service providers under strict contracts; (c) regulators or in response to lawful requests; (d) parties to whom you direct us. We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

Under GLBA you may limit certain sharing. To opt out, email privacy@suncredit.com. California, Vermont, and other state residents have additional rights described in our state notices.

Security

We use 256-bit encryption in transit and at rest, multi-factor authentication, role-based access controls, and continuous monitoring (SOC 2 Type II).
